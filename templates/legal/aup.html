This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the rules, guidelines, and restrictions for using FeVid.cloud ("FeVid", "we", "us", or "our"). Our platform is built to provide creators with a censorship-resistant, cryptocurrency-native infrastructure to monetize explicit and non-explicit digital content.

We believe in freedom of expression among consenting adults. However, to maintain the safety of our users, creators, and the platform infrastructure, we strictly enforce the following boundaries. By accessing or using FeVid, you agree to abide by this policy.

1. The Core Requirement: Age & Consent

The foundational rule of FeVid is absolute. Every individual depicted in any explicit content on this platform must be at least 18 years of age (or the age of majority in their jurisdiction) and must have given explicit, verifiable consent to be filmed and distributed.

Creators are solely responsible for maintaining records of age and consent for all participants in their content.

2. Strictly Prohibited Content (Zero Tolerance)

Uploading, requesting, or distributing any of the following material is strictly prohibited. Violating these rules will result in immediate, permanent account termination without notice, forfeiture of pending payouts, and reporting to relevant international law enforcement agencies (such as NCMEC or Europol).

You may not upload or distribute:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM): Any content depicting, encouraging, or promoting the sexual exploitation of minors. This includes realistic illustrations, AI-generated depictions, or "lolicon/shotacon" material.
  • Non-Consensual Content: Any material recorded or distributed without the explicit consent of the depicted individuals. This includes "revenge porn," hidden camera recordings (creeping/upskirt), or stolen content.
  • Bestiality & Animal Abuse: Any sexual acts involving animals, or content depicting cruelty or violence toward animals.
  • Extreme Violence & Gore: Content depicting real-world murder, torture, snuff, severe bodily mutilation, or necrophilia.
  • Self-Harm: Content that encourages, promotes, or graphically depicts suicide, severe self-harm, or eating disorders.
  • Illegal Services Facilitation: Using the platform to advertise, facilitate, or negotiate real-world illegal acts, including prostitution/escorting, sex trafficking, or the sale of illegal drugs and firearms.

3. Allowed Content

Because we utilize decentralized payment networks, we do not restrict content based on the "brand safety" guidelines of legacy financial institutions. Provided all participants are consenting adults (18+) and the content does not violate Section 2 of this policy, FeVid permits a wide range of material, including:

  • Solo and partnered explicit sexual acts.
  • BDSM, fetish, and kink content (provided it does not cross into extreme, life-threatening violence or non-consensual harm).
  • Roleplay scenarios (Note: Any content simulating non-consensual acts must clearly be labeled and recognizable as fictional roleplay between consenting adults).
  • Audio-only erotica and adult digital art/animations (excluding those depicting minors).

4. Platform & Network Integrity

FeVid is built for high performance. To protect our infrastructure and ensure fair access for all users, you agree not to engage in technical or financial abuse.

You may not:

  • Scrape or Pirate: Use automated bots, scripts, or unauthorized software to rip, download, or distribute streaming video content hosted on FeVid.cloud.
  • Spam: Send unsolicited mass messages, deceptive links, or promotional spam to Creators or Subscribers.
  • Money Laundering: Use the platform's cryptocurrency infrastructure primarily as a "mixer" or tumbler to obfuscate the origin of illicit funds, rather than for the legitimate purchase of Creator content.
  • Attack the Infrastructure: Attempt to bypass security protocols, reverse-engineer our APIs, or launch Denial of Service (DoS) attacks against our servers.

5. Enforcement & Appeals

We reserve the right to review flagged content, investigate violations, and take appropriate action at our sole discretion.

  • Actions We May Take: We may remove content, suspend your account, or permanently ban your access to FeVid.cloud.
  • Forfeiture: Accounts terminated for Zero Tolerance violations (Section 2) will forfeit any pending cryptocurrency balances, which may be seized or used to cover investigative costs.
  • Appeals: If you believe your content was removed or your account was suspended in error, you may submit an appeal to our support team.
