FeVid ("we," "us," or "our") operates as a crypto-native platform. All financial transactions on FeVid.cloud—including subscription purchases, pay-per-view access, and creator tipping—are processed exclusively via decentralized digital asset networks (such as Bitcoin, Monero, Bitcoin Cash, Solana, and Tron).

By executing a transaction on our platform, you acknowledge that you understand the mechanics of blockchain networks and agree to the financial terms outlined below.

1. Blockchain Irreversibility & The Absence of Chargebacks

Unlike legacy web applications utilizing traditional credit cards or fiat payment processors, FeVid relies entirely on peer-to-peer decentralized networks.

  • Finality: Once a cryptocurrency transaction is broadcasted to its respective network and achieves confirmation on the blockchain, it is mathematically immutable. It cannot be reversed, canceled, or undone by FeVid, any creator, or any centralized entity.
  • No Chargebacks: Because there are no banks or credit card issuers mediating transactions, there is no mechanism for chargebacks on this platform. You cannot open a payment dispute through a third-party financial service.

2. Refund Limitations (All Sales Are Final)

Because digital video content is immediately accessible and consumable upon successful blockchain confirmation, FeVid enforces a strict All Sales Are Final policy.

  • Standard Purchases: All crypto payments made to access content, unlock pay-per-view materials, or tip creators are non-refundable.
  • Creator Autonomy: FeVid does not possess the authority to unilaterally claw back distributed funds from a Creator's internal balance. If a Subscriber is deeply unsatisfied with a purchase, any potential compensation is left entirely to the sole discretion of the individual Creator, provided the funds have not yet been withdrawn from the platform.
  • Technical Failures: In the rare event of a severe platform server failure where a confirmed payment was successfully verified on-chain but the system completely failed to grant access to the digital asset, users may contact nakatamodem@disroot.org within forty-eight (48) hours with their transaction hash (TXID) for manual system resolution.

3. Network Fees & Address Accuracy

When executing payments or processing creator payouts, the technical execution relies heavily on user accuracy.

  • Critical Risk Warning: Cryptocurrency transactions sent to an incorrect address, an unsupported network, or omitting a required destination tag/memo (where applicable) are permanently and irreversibly lost. FeVid cannot recover these assets.
  • Network (Gas/Miner) Fees: Every transaction on a blockchain requires a network fee paid to validators or miners. These external fees fluctuate based on network congestion and are entirely independent of FeVid. Network fees incurred while making a purchase or executing a payout withdrawal are borne solely by the user.
  • Price Volatility: Digital asset values change rapidly. The price displayed at checkout is fixed for a limited time window to allow for network transmission. If a payment is sent late and the value of the asset drops significantly before entering our hot wallets, the transaction may fail to register automatically, requiring manual support intervention.

4. Creator Earnings & Platform Fee Deductions

For individuals utilizing the platform to monetize content:

  • Platform Split: FeVid retains a fixed platform infrastructure fee of [X]% from every completed inbound transaction. The remaining balance is immediately credited to the Creator's internal platform ledger.
  • Minimum Payout Thresholds: To prevent micro-transactions from being completely consumed by blockchain network fees, Creators may only initiate an on-chain withdrawal once their accumulated balance meets the minimum threshold defined in the Creator Dashboard for that specific asset.
  • Network Fee Deductions on Payouts: When a Creator requests a withdrawal, the network's native transaction fee required to push the asset from our secure storage to the Creator's destination wallet will be cleanly deducted from the total payout amount.
